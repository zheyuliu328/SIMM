Key Components of SIMM Model Validation

Slide 2 | High-Information Consulting Layout | Aligned with ISDA SIMM v2.8+2506 and HKMA CR-G-14
Purpose of this phase: validate whether Financial Institution A's SIMM implementation in Internal System B is methodologically correct, data-sound, and regulatorily defensible, using independent evidence and variance attribution.

1. Validation Scope Architecture

  • Coverage principle: one representative trade per risk type, stratified by product type, currency, and option moneyness (ATM/ITM/OTM).
  • Risk sensitivity coverage: Delta, Vega, Curvature, with Base Correlation where applicable.
  • Population strategy: sampled deep-dive + full-population reconciliation.
  • Data perimeter: trade/position, market data, reference data, mappings, and aggregation outputs.
  • Out-of-scope clarification: no independent rebuild of yield curves/volatility surface calibration unless separately commissioned.
  • Governance objective: produce audit-ready evidence (requirement → test → evidence → conclusion).

Control Dimensions

  • Regulatory compliance
  • Calculation logic accuracy
  • Data quality and lineage governance
2
Methodology & Sensitivity Validation
SIMM Methodology Implementation
  • Correct SIMM version and effective parameter set.
  • Risk weights and correlation matrices applied correctly.
  • Aggregation logic by risk/product class validated.
  • No prohibited diversification benefits.
Risk Sensitivity Verification
  • Delta/Vega/Curvature generation consistent with specs.
  • Bump methodology and optionality handling reviewed.
  • Correct bucket mapping by currency/tenor/issuer/sector.
  • Consistency between pricing model output and SIMM sensitivities.
3
Data, Recalculation, and Outcome Analysis
Data Quality & Reconciliation
  • Completeness/accuracy checks for sampled trades.
  • Full-population reconciliation to GL, sub-ledger, golden source.
  • Curve and vol-surface identifier consistency between valuation and SIMM.
  • Stale/missing data handling controls assessed.
Independent Recompute & Backtesting Review
  • Independent SIMM recomputation at component and total IM levels.
  • Variance analysis with root-cause attribution.
  • Review of 1-day, 10-day, and rolling backtesting methodology.
  • Material findings linked to remediation actions and retest criteria.
Expected output of this phase: validated methodology implementation, evidence-based variance conclusions, and a regulator-ready issue/remediation log supporting final compliance judgement.
