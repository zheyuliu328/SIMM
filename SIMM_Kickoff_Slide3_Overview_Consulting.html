Overview of SIMM Model Validation Approach

Slide 3 | 4 Sections + Goal | Max 3 core points per section
Framework intent: provide a concise, regulator-aligned execution map from data validation to final reporting conclusions.
1
Data Input Validation
  1. Validate transaction data completeness and key-field consistency for in-scope portfolios.
  2. Confirm market data timeliness and source integrity across rates, volatilities, and FX inputs.
  3. Verify reference-data mappings (bucket, issuer, tenor, currency) used by the SIMM engine.
2
Sensitivity & Risk Validation
  1. Verify Delta, Vega, and Curvature are generated with correct model conventions and shocks.
  2. Confirm sensitivities are mapped to the correct risk factors and buckets.
  3. Check optionality and concentration treatments are applied consistently with methodology rules.
3
Standardized Approach Validation
  1. Recompute SIMM independently using the approved version and parameter set.
  2. Validate risk weights, correlations, and aggregation logic at component and total IM levels.
  3. Investigate material variances using structured root-cause attribution.
4
Reporting Validation
  1. Assess outcomes against ISDA SIMM and HKMA CR-G-14 expectations.
  2. Classify findings as compliant, partial, or gap with clear evidence references.
  3. Assign remediation owner, target timeline, and retest criteria for closure.
Goal: Deliver an audit-ready and regulator-aligned validation conclusion supported by transparent evidence and actionable remediation.
