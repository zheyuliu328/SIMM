Project Governance and Engagement

We have designed a governance framework to ensure project progress, key observations, and validation outcomes are communicated to stakeholders in a timely and transparent manner.
Bi-weekly progress updates
Ad-hoc interviews / meetings
Bi-weekly Progress Update Meetings
  • Communicate progress status, preliminary findings, and key issues identified.
  • Align timeline, dependencies, and delivery expectations with all core stakeholders.
  • Share testing procedures and evidence basis supporting interim conclusions.
  • Escalate roadblocks requiring management decisions or cross-team support.
  • Capture feedback and confirm next-step priorities and action ownership.
Ad-hoc Interviews / Working Meetings
  • Collect targeted inputs from business, risk, model, data, and IT teams for deliverable completion.
  • Maintain regular issue-resolution dialogue to close data, mapping, and methodology gaps.
